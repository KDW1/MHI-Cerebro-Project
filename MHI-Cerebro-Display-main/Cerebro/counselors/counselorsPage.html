Scheduling an Appointment, Making a Message

  1. Copy the following text...

    "Hi,
    I'd like to schedule an appointment to talk about some concerns that i'm having. Could you help me find a time to talk with you?
    Thanks, Your Name    "

  2. Click on the button underneath your counselor.
  3. Click the message button in the top right of their Schoology profile.
  4. Copy the text into a Schoology message.
