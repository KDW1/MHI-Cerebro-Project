The Cerebro Goal

Click to edit text

Cerebro is a mental health program for students at Minnetonka Middle School East. It is devoted to providing help for students that is approachable and comfortable. Cerebro is primarily focused on three main areas.

  1. Guiding students to mental health resources and coping mechanisms for when things get tough. 

  2. Peer mentorship, for when a counselor or teacher just doesn't cut it. And, a high school student is so much easier to talk to. 

  3. Connecting you with our top notch MME counselors.
